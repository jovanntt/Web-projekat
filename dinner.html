WHAT IS "WAGYU STEAK"?

"Wagyu" is the japanese word for beef. "Wa" means japanese
style and "gyu" means beef. Kobe is the region where the Wagyu
breed and feeding techniques for Wagyu cattle originated.
Wagyu is a pure japanese breed with records dating back to
1830. It is widely recognized as the best quality of beef due to its
high degree of marbling which gives tenderness, juiciness, and
flavor unrivaled by any other cattle anywhere in the world.
Wagyu combines the USDA scale and the strict Japanese scale
of grading Kobe beef to create a SUPER Prime grade. The
ambiance and mystique are nearly as important in the eating
experience as they melt in your mouth with flavor and
tenderness.
